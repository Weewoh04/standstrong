Why exercise is the #1 treatment for POTS

Research from Vanderbilt University and the Mayo Clinic consistently shows that a structured, progressive exercise program is the most effective long-term intervention for POTS — surpassing medications for many patients. The key is starting slowly in recumbent positions and never pushing through syncope symptoms.
